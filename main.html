About
“In times of disaster, every second counts.”

In times of disaster, every second counts. Yet, response agencies often struggle with the overwhelming task of sifting through vast amounts of information to identify critical, actionable data. Social media platforms, news portals, and other open sources offer a wealth of real-time information, but manually monitoring and categorizing this data is both inefficient and time-consuming.

Innovize was created to solve this pressing challenge. Our advanced software solution is designed to efficiently aggregate and categorize disaster-related data from a variety of sources, providing disaster response agencies with the timely and specific information they need to act swiftly and effectively.
